Will Ferrell

Will Ferrell is one of the funniest actors we have come to know and has starred in 'Elf', 'Step Brothers', and 'Anchorman'. He has delivered several classic performance that will never be forgotten and will live on in film history forever (especially Elf).

Bryan Cranston

Bryan Cranston is a well accoladed actor that starred in the hit shows 'Breaking Bad' and 'Malcolm in the Middle'.

Vincent D'Onofrio

Vince D'Onofrio is a well known actor that appeared in 'Full Metal Jacket', 'Daredevil', and 'Men in Black'. Vince has been known to play very menacing characters and his characters have typically met a very tragic end.

John Carroll Lynch

img src= "https://factsbuddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/John-Carroll-Lynch.jpg" width= "350" height= "250" alt= "J. Carroll Lynch" title= "J. Carroll Lynch"/>

John Carroll Lynch, who is an actor most known for his roles in 'The Founder', 'Zodiac', and 'American Horror Story'. Lynch is often typecasted as either a put together organized man, or an insane sociopathic serial killer which makes sense. Lynch does a great job of expressing emotions while being portrayed as an enemy and usually does a great job hooking viewers in with his dialogue.
