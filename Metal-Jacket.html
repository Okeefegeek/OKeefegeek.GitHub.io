Movie Review: Full Metal Jacket

In Stanley Kubrick's critically acclaimed war film 'Full Metal Jacket' we follow the story of a lone soldier named James who early on in the movie is deemed with the nickname Joker. We start this movie in Bootcamp and we end in the very middle of the heated Vietnam war. Joker goes from the worst thing in the world being his Drill Seargent's voice to commonly being shot at and watching his friends dying. This movie does a beautiful job at painting the horrors of War in a way that was allowed to be showed on the big screen.
